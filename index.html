    Kang Jin

    Kang.Jin at cchmc.org [Github] [Google Scholar] [Twitter]

    Hi, I am a Ph.D. candidate in Biomedical Informatics Division under the supervision of Prof. Bruce Aronow at Cincinnati Children’s Hospital. I have developed CellDrift, a probabilistic model to study the temporal perturbational patterns and in single-cell experiments, as well as ToppCell, a user-friendly framework to disentangle multiplexed gene signatures in complex single-cell data. I’m also actively involved in single-cell analysis across multiple tissues, including cancer, lung, immune systems, and brain organoids. I aim to improve our understanding of emerging spatial transcriptomics and multi-omics data with the development of innovative computational methods.
